Satellite Data Finder

Search for scenes and scans from a range of Earth Observation data sets held in the CEDA archive. Refine your search with geospatial, temporal and satellite filters. The search currently supports Sentinel 1, 2 and 3, and Landsat 5, 7 and 8.

Use the filters under the "Explore Data" tab to narrow down your search.

Temporal Filter

The temporal filter allows you to select a time window of interest.

Satellite Filter

Use the Satellite Filter to selecte specific missions.
